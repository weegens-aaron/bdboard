# How to pour a formula

## What You'll Learn

How to open the formula picker, choose a formula, fill in its variables, and pour
a structured batch of related work items onto the board in one go.

## Prerequisites

- bdboard running with your project open — see the
  [Quick Start](../Getting-Started/QuickStart.md).
- At least one formula available in your project.

## Overview

A **formula** is a template for a whole set of related items. Instead of creating
them one at a time, you pick a formula, fill in a few blanks, and bdboard creates
the entire batch — correctly linked — at once. This is the fastest way to spin up
a repeatable chunk of work.

## Step 1: Open the formula dialog

In the top bar, click **+ Pour Formula**.

**Expected result:** a dialog opens and loads the list of available formulas.

## Step 2: Pick a formula

Choose a formula from the list.

**Expected result:** the dialog shows that formula's name, a short description,
and a form for any variables it needs.

> [!TIP]
> Want to see what it will create first? Expand **Show all steps** to preview
> every item the formula will pour, with its title and type.

## Step 3: Fill in the variables

Fill each field. Required fields are marked and must be completed; fields with a
sensible default come pre-filled, and you can change them.

**Expected result:** every required field has a value, and the **Pour onto
board** button is ready to use.

> [!NOTE]
> Some formulas take no variables at all — in that case you'll just see a note
> saying so, and you can pour straight away.

## Step 4: Pour

Click **Pour onto board**.

**Expected result:** a confirmation appears saying how many items were added, and
the new items show up on the board automatically — no reload needed. The picker
resets so you can pour another if you like.

> [!WARNING]
> If you see a **partial pour** warning instead of a clean confirmation, only
> some of the items landed. Remove the incomplete batch before retrying so you
> don't end up with duplicates.

## Step 5: Find your new work

Close the dialog and look at the board. The new items appear in their lanes,
highest-priority and newest first.

**Expected result:** the poured items are visible on the board, ready to pick up.
See [View your work on the board](ViewingYourWork.md).

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| The formula list is empty | Your project has no formulas available yet. |
| The Pour button won't activate | A required field is empty — fill every marked field. |
| Partial pour warning | Remove the incomplete batch, then pour again. |
| I don't see the new items | They arrive live; if needed, switch to the board tab — it updates on its own. |
